Welcome to WeilerBrau Brewing

Established in 1991 by Thomas, William, and Michael Weiler in Downers Grove, Illinois. For over three decades, we've been crafting small-batch brews with passion and proud family tradition.





Meet The Brewer: Thomas founded WeilerBrau Brewing with his brother in 1991, beginning with kit brews on the kitchen stove. Over the past 30+ years he has mastered the craft of all grain brewing incorporating home grown and dried hops. Tom brews a wide range of styles, including pale ales, English bitters, stouts, and more.
